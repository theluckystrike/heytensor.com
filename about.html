About HeyTensor

Our mission and the network of AI resources we contribute to.

Our Mission

HeyTensor exists because deep learning education should not require a PhD to understand. Too many resources assume advanced mathematical background, skip crucial implementation details, or present theoretical concepts without connecting them to practical code. Our mission is to make deep learning genuinely accessible by providing tutorials that explain the intuition behind every concept, show complete working code for every technique, and progress logically from fundamentals to advanced topics without skipping steps.

Every tutorial on HeyTensor is written with the philosophy that true understanding comes from building things yourself. Rather than presenting neural networks as black boxes with mysterious internals, we walk you through implementing each component from scratch so you understand exactly what happens during forward propagation, backpropagation, and optimization. Once you have built it yourself, using high-level frameworks becomes vastly more effective because you understand what is happening behind the abstractions.

HeyTensor is part of a network of specialized AI and machine learning resources. Our flagship resource, zovo.one, provides a comprehensive AI tools and resources platform that complements the educational focus of HeyTensor with production-oriented tooling and model evaluation resources.
