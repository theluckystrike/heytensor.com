About HeyTensor

What is HeyTensor?

HeyTensor is a free tensor shape calculator designed for PyTorch developers. It lets you chain operations like Conv2d, Linear, MaxPool2d, Flatten, Reshape, Transpose, and Concatenate to see how tensor shapes propagate through your model. Shape mismatches are highlighted immediately, saving you from cryptic runtime errors.

Tensor shape errors are the most common debugging problem in deep learning. Instead of running code, waiting for an error, and mentally computing shapes, HeyTensor gives you instant visual feedback as you design your architecture.

Supported Operations

HeyTensor currently supports the following PyTorch operations with full shape computation:

  • Conv2d — 2D convolution with configurable output channels, kernel size, stride, and padding
  • Linear — Fully connected layer (transforms last dimension)
  • MaxPool2d — 2D max pooling with kernel size, stride, and padding
  • BatchNorm — Batch normalization (shape unchanged)
  • Flatten — Flatten dimensions from start_dim onward
  • Reshape — Arbitrary reshape with -1 dimension inference
  • Transpose — Swap two dimensions
  • Concatenate — Concatenate with another tensor along a dimension
